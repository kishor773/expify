Saving money is the foundation of financial stability and independence. It involves setting aside a portion of your income for future needs, emergencies, or planned expenses.

Investing is a powerful way to grow wealth over time and achieve financial goals. It involves putting money into assets like stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or real estate, aiming for higher returns than savings accounts.

Tracking money ensures you stay on top of your finances and meet your financial goals. It involves monitoring your income, expenses, and investments to make informed decisions.
